## 🚀 Who we are

Arkanis Corporation is a Star Citizen community organization and a small open-source dev crew. We started as friends adventuring through Stanton and grew into something bigger: a family of pilots, traders, explorers — and the engineers building tools to make the game smoother for everyone.

We believe gaming is best enjoyed together. Our org is built on three principles: **inclusivity** (every skill level welcome), **collaboration** (shared adventures, shared wins), and **respect** (have fun, without ruining someone else's).

Our development philosophy is just as simple:

> **Minimize friction. Maximize immersion.**

Whether you're hauling cargo across Pyro, hunting hangar cards in Stanton, or shipping .NET services on the side — there's a place for you here.

## 🛠️ Our open-source projects

### 🌠 [Arkanis Overlay](https://github.com/ArkanisCorporation/ArkanisOverlay) — [arkanis.cc/overlay](https://arkanis.cc/overlay)

The next-generation in-game companion for Star Citizen. Search items, ships, commodities, and locations without alt-tabbing — with live data from our [UEX Corporation](https://uexcorp.space) partnership. Read-only and anti-cheat friendly. Built in **C# / .NET 8** with **WPF + Blazor + ASP.NET Core**. Free, open-source (PolyForm Noncommercial), and self-updating. Try the [in-browser demo](https://overlay.arkanis.cc) or grab the [latest release](https://github.com/ArkanisCorporation/ArkanisOverlay/releases/latest).

### 🔐 [Hosting.Extensions.1Password](https://github.com/ArkanisCorporation/Hosting.Extensions.1Password) — [NuGet](https://www.nuget.org/packages/Arkanis.Hosting.Extensions.1Password)

A drop-in `IConfiguration` bridge for **.NET** developers who want to keep `op://` secret references inside `appsettings.json` (and friends) without ever committing the secrets themselves. One line in startup, zero changes to your existing configuration code. Includes first-class **.NET Aspire** integration with parameter resources for distributed apps. Built on .NET Standard 2.1, MIT-licensed.

### 🏗️ [Exec-Hangar](https://github.com/ArkanisCorporation/Exec-Hangar) — [exec.arkanis.cc](https://exec.arkanis.cc)

A lightweight front-end tracker for the **PYAM Executive Hangar** rotation in Star Citizen — accurate cadence, live ONLINE/OFFLINE state, three-day forecast, and self timers for Checkmate, Orbituary, Ruin Station, and supervisor routes. Pure **JavaScript / HTML / CSS**, runs locally in your browser, MIT-licensed. Maintained by [@NBDBatman](https://github.com/NBDBatman) / MercuryHQ, building on Xyxyll's original tracker.
